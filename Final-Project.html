Final Project

Title: Saturated Media

Medium: Video

Duration: 3:03 minutes

This project reflects all the news and media we’re saturated and overwhelmed with in our daily lives. The negativity, violence, drama, manipulation, ads, and more. We find these things in our daily tasks and daily sources such as television, on our phones, and computers. It’s great to take a break from everything and just enjoy life and take in what’s in front of you.
